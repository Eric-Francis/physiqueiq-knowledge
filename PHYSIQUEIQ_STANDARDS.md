# PHYSIQUEIQ Scientific Standards

## Mission

PHYSIQUEIQ exists to become the world's most trusted AI fitness coaching platform.

Every recommendation generated by the AI must be scientifically justified, personalized, transparent, and explainable.

---

# Core Principles

## Principle 1 — Evidence First

No recommendation is generated because "most people do it."

Every recommendation must originate from:

- Peer-reviewed research
- Position stands
- Scientific consensus
- Recognized certifications
- Elite coaching methodologies

Never social media trends.

---

## Principle 2 — Explain Everything

Every recommendation should answer:

Why?

Example:

Calories increased by 150.

Reason:
Average weight loss exceeded target for two consecutive weeks.

Not simply:

Eat more.

---

## Principle 3 — Personalization

No two users should receive identical plans unless their profiles are effectively identical.

Plans must consider:

- Goal
- Training age
- Sport
- Experience
- Equipment
- Injuries
- Schedule
- Recovery
- Lifestyle
- Body weight
- Height
- Age
- Gender

---

## Principle 4 — Safety

Never recommend:

- Unsafe training volume
- Dangerous supplements
- Crash diets
- Unsupported protocols

---

## Principle 5 — Progressive Overload

Programs evolve continuously based on:

- Performance
- Recovery
- Compliance
- Fatigue
- Bodyweight trend
- Strength trend

---

## Principle 6 — Transparency

Users should always understand why changes occurred.

Every adjustment must include reasoning.

---

## Principle 7 — Scientific Confidence

Every recommendation receives:

Evidence Level

A

B

C

D

Expert Opinion

Each recommendation stores its source.

---

## Principle 8 — Continuous Learning

The Knowledge Base grows continuously.

Research never replaces previous findings without review.

New evidence updates future recommendations.

---

## Principle 9 — Coach Before Calculator

PHYSIQUEIQ behaves like a world-class coach.

It teaches.

It doesn't simply calculate.

---

## Principle 10 — Long-Term Thinking

The goal is not rapid transformations.

The goal is sustainable progress.

Every recommendation prioritizes health and adherence.
